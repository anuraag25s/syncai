# Product Requirements Document — SyncAI

## Problem Statement
Remote teams rely on daily standups for alignment, but these meetings are costly, repetitive, and poorly suited for async work.

Teams need alignment **without forcing everyone into a call**.

---

## Goals
- Replace daily standups with async updates
- Improve visibility into blockers and dependencies
- Reduce meeting time without losing clarity
- Ground updates in real work data

---

## Non-Goals
- Full project management replacement
- Real-time chat or video meetings
- Performance monitoring or surveillance

---

## Target Users
Primary:
- Engineering Managers
- Product Managers
- Tech Leads

Secondary:
- Founders
- Fully remote startups (10–80 people)

---

## Core Workflow

1. Daily async check-in prompt
2. Team members respond (text or voice)
3. SyncAI enriches updates with work context
4. AI generates structured outputs
5. Digests delivered to team & individuals

---

## MVP Features

Must-have:
- Slack bot
- 3-question daily check-in
- AI-generated team digest
- Blocker detection
- Action items with owners

Nice-to-have:
- Voice input
- Jira/GitHub integration
- Personalized digests

---

## Success Criteria
- High participation rate
- Clear blocker identification
- Teams choosing async over meetings
